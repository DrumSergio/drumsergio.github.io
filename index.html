About Me

Sergio is an enthusiastic DevOps Engineer with almost 6 years of experience in the IT sector. He enjoys with the research, architecture, development, deployment and maintenance of all sorts of projects, with a special focus on process automation. He defines himself as a tireless, respectful, responsible, tenacious, and patient person. He excels at delivery, communication, and research.
