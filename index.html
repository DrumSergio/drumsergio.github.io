About Me

Sergio is an enthusiastic Telecommunications Engineer with over 5 years of experience in the telecommunications field. He enjoys with the research, architecture, development, deployment and maintenance of all sorts of projects, with a special focus on process automation. He defines himself as a tireless, respectful, responsible, tenacious, and patient person. He excels at delivery, communication, and research. He embraces a “Never Stop Learning” attitude, ready for the next challenge. He is not a "superstar engineer" nor a "project hoarder", but a collaborative engineer that always looks the common good of all parties, even if that means working in areas where other teammates do not want to get involved in. He looks for positions with a balanced degree of responsibility, as he highly appreciates a balanced work/life relationship.
